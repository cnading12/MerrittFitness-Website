Let's Promote Your Event Together

Collaborative Marketing for Public Events

Hello, and thank you for booking a public event at Merritt Wellness. Because your event is open to the community, we'd love to help you spread the word — at no extra cost — as part of a collaborative marketing effort. So we can coordinate with you directly, please send your materials (and your direct email and phone number, if you haven't already) to manager@merrittwellness.net or call/text 720-357-9499.

What We Offer

  • A printed flyer hung on the community bulletin board in our wellness space.
  • A feature on the "Upcoming Events" tab of our website.
  • Social media support — we're happy to help advertise your event across our channels.

What We Need From You

To execute the three methods above, please email the following to manager@merrittwellness.net:

1. For the Bulletin-Board Flyer

A print-ready PDF of your flyer that we can print and hang in our wellness space.

2. For Our Website's "Upcoming Events" Tab

  • An event description
  • Your social media handles
  • A link to purchase tickets (how customers can buy/register)
  • An event image meeting the specs below
Image specs:
  • Aspect ratio 16:10 — most important; off-ratio images get cropped. 1600×1000px or larger (the site downscales).
  • JPG or PNG are both fine, and keep the subject centered — we center-crop, and edges may trim on narrow screens.

3. For Social Media Promotion

Either tag us as a collaborator on your post, or send us the content you'd like shared and we'll post it to our social media.

The sooner we receive these, the more runway we have to promote your event. Email your materials to manager@merrittwellness.net or call 720-357-9499 with any questions — we're excited to help make your event a success.
